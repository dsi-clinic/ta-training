# Communication

## Professional Expectations
- Communicate with students respectfully and constructively.  
- Use official channels (Canvas, Slack, or email) for project-related matters.  
- Respond promptly to student questions, within reason.  

## With Students
- Clarify that TA sessions are for guidance, not for doing their work.  
- Be approachable, but maintain professional boundaries.  
- Encourage students to ask questions and raise concerns early.  

## With Mentors and Clinic Director
- Share updates on group progress or issues.  
- Report any incidents of unprofessional behavior immediately.  
- Do not resolve disputes over grades alone — escalate when necessary.  

## Common Scenarios
- **Student absence**: Allow Zoom participation if illness is reported, and direct them to notify the clinic director.  
- **Grade disputes**: Refer students to the clinic director, documenting attendance or grading details.  
- **Unprofessional behavior**: Report quickly to mentor and clinic director.
