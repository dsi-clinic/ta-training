# TA Role and Expectations

This section covers the **teaching, professionalism, and day-to-day responsibilities** of a TA in the Data Science Clinic.

## Purpose
- Provide TAs and mentors with a reference for **effective teaching and student interaction**.
- Support consistency in **expectations, communication, and grading standards** across projects.  
- Equip TAs to give **professional guidance and maintain appropriate boundaries**.

## How to Use This Section
- Use these documents when preparing to **interact with students or plan TA sessions**.  
- Refer back when you need **guidance on boundaries, communication, or grading** (e.g., how to handle difficult conversations or escalate issues).  
- Use these standards to **maintain consistency and professionalism** across all student interactions.

## Key Principles
- **Enforce, don't code**: TAs guide students to solve issues, not fix repos themselves.  
- **Consistency**: Apply the same standards across projects so grading is fair.  
- **Professionalism**: Model how real data science teams work.  
- **Escalation**: Raise systemic or recurring issues with mentors and the Clinic Director.
