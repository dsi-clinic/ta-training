# Professionalism

## Expectations for TAs
- Act as role models for students in professional conduct.  
- Uphold academic integrity and ethical standards.  
- Maintain neutrality in grading and student disputes.  
- Balance supportiveness with accountability.  

## Expectations for Students
- Attend and participate in all required sessions.  
- Communicate respectfully with peers, TAs, mentors, and clients.  
- Meet deadlines and follow project workflows.  
- Treat the Clinic as a professional client engagement.  

## TA Role in Enforcing Professionalism
- Address small issues directly with students during sessions.  
- Document concerns clearly.  
- Escalate repeated or serious issues to mentors or the clinic director.  

## Common Situations
- **Unprofessional behavior in session**: Stop the behavior and report it.  
- **Students asking you to do their work**: Decline and report to mentor/director.  
- **Confidential information**: Do not share outside of official channels.
